Summary

Dawns of Essence Salon is a full service multicultural beauty salon that specializes in ethnic hair care.

Our beauty salon is dedicated to constantly providing high customer service. We are known for using the best high quality products in the Metro Atlanta Area and for being an enjoyable salon with not only a great atomosphere, but a friendly staff.

Dawns of Essence Salon is an establishment located in northern Metro Atlanta. We are located in Lawrenceville near Grayson, Duluth, Snellville, Loganville, Swuanee, Winder, Brasselton,Hamilton Mill, Sugar Hill,Budford, Norcross, Lilburn within metro Georgia area.

What We Do?

  • Dawns of Essence Salon: Specializes in Short/Medium/and Long Hair Styles.
  • Designer Haircuts for Men, Women and Children.
  • Ear-piercing/Nose-piercing/ Facial Waxing.
  • Roller wraps/Dubi wraps/Up-Do's.
  • Hair Coloring/Highlight/Hair tinting.
  • Hair Weaves/Quick-weaves/Lace fronts/Hair infusions/Extensions.
  • Hair Relaxers/Perms/Curly perms.
  • Hair Braiding- Tree/ Micro/ Single or Double Strand.
